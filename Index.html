Insurance News & Analysis

Dowling IBNR Weekly Summary: February 24, 2025

Published on February 26, 2025

This week's Dowling report highlights significant developments in the reinsurance market, with property cat rates showing signs of stabilization after two years of hardening. The report also notes increased interest from capital markets in insurance-linked securities (ILS), potentially indicating a shift in market dynamics.

Key points:

  • Property cat rate increases slowing to single digits
  • New capital entering the ILS market
  • Technology investment in cat modeling showing promising results

Our analysis: The cooling rate environment presents both challenges and opportunities for insurtech companies focused on property risk. We're seeing potential for partnerships between traditional carriers and AI-powered startups that can provide more granular risk assessment.

Specialty Insurance Market Update: Cyber Trends

Published on February 22, 2025

The cyber insurance market continues to evolve rapidly with claims frequency increasing while severity has somewhat stabilized. New AI-powered security tools are beginning to impact underwriting models, with carriers increasingly differentiating based on adoption of these technologies.

We're closely monitoring how large language models are being deployed for both attack and defense in the cybersecurity landscape, with implications for coverage terms and pricing models.

AI Initiatives Update: Claims Automation Pilot

Our claims automation project with PartnerCo has completed its first phase, processing over 5,000 auto claims with impressive results:

  • 76% of claims handled without human intervention
  • Average processing time reduced from 3.2 days to 6.4 hours
  • Customer satisfaction scores increased by 12 points

Phase two will expand to homeowners claims starting next month, with a focus on water damage incidents initially.
